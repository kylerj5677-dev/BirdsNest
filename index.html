About

Birds Nest (now known as Birdsnest Eats & Ales) is a local favorite bar & grill in Florence, SC. Known for its cozy atmosphere, strong drinks, and standout burgers, it features indoor seating, a lively bar, and a popular outdoor patio.

Whether you're grabbing lunch, watching the game, or enjoying a night out, Birds Nest offers a laid-back vibe with unforgettable food.
